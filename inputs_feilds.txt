nput Fields Explanation (13 Features)
1. Age

English: Age of the patient in years.
Telugu: Patient vayasu (samvatsaralu lo).

Example: 45, 52, 60

2. Sex

English: Gender of the patient.
Telugu: Patient gender.

Values:

0 → Female

1 → Male

3. Chest Pain Type (cp)

English: Type of chest pain experienced by the patient.
Telugu: Patient ki unna chest pain type.

Values:

0 → Typical Angina

1 → Atypical Angina

2 → Non-anginal Pain

3 → Asymptomatic

4. Resting Blood Pressure (trestbps)

English: Blood pressure of the patient at rest (mm Hg).
Telugu: Patient rest lo unna BP value.

Example: 120, 130, 140

5. Cholesterol (chol)

English: Cholesterol level in blood (mg/dl).
Telugu: Blood lo cholesterol amount.

Example: 200, 240, 300

6. Fasting Blood Sugar (fbs)

English: Blood sugar level after fasting.

Values:

0 → Less than 120 mg/dl

1 → Greater than 120 mg/dl

Telugu: Fasting taruvatha blood sugar level.

7. Resting ECG Results (restecg)

English: Electrocardiographic results showing heart electrical activity.
Telugu: ECG test results.

Values:

0 → Normal

1 → ST-T wave abnormality

2 → Left ventricular hypertrophy

8. Maximum Heart Rate Achieved (thalach)

English: Highest heart rate recorded during exercise.
Telugu: Exercise time lo maximum heart beat rate.

Example: 150, 170, 180

9. Exercise Induced Angina (exang)

English: Chest pain caused by exercise.

Values:

0 → No

1 → Yes

Telugu: Exercise chesina appudu chest pain vachinda leda.

10. Oldpeak

English: ST depression caused by exercise compared to rest.
Telugu: Exercise valla heart activity lo change.

Example: 1.0, 2.3, 3.5

11. Slope

English: Slope of peak exercise ST segment.
Telugu: Exercise peak time lo ECG slope.

Values:

0 → Upsloping

1 → Flat

2 → Downsloping

12. CA

English: Number of major blood vessels colored by fluoroscopy.

Values:

0 – 3

Telugu: Heart blood vessels count.

13. Thal

English: Thalassemia blood disorder test result.

Values:

0 → Normal

1 → Fixed defect

2 → Reversible defect

3 → Other condition

Telugu: Blood disorder test result.

Output Field
Target

English: Indicates whether the person has heart disease or not.

Values:

0 → No Heart Disease

1 → Heart Disease

Telugu: Patient ki heart disease unda leda ani chupisthundi.
